SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

The Logic of Law.

We do not rely on subjective opinion. Our process is a rigid, three-stage forensic audit that compares the "Ideal" (The Law) against the "Reality" (Your Evidence).

STAGE 1

The Legislative Framework

Before we look at your case, the system must understand the rules. Our engine maintains a live, indexed library of UK Statutes, Building Regulations, and Case Law precedents.

This acts as the "Control Data". It establishes exactly what the other party should have done to be legally compliant.

> INDEXING: Consumer Rights Act 2015...

> INDEXING: Employment Rights Act 1996...

> INDEXING: Building Regulations Part P...

[STATUS: STANDARD ESTABLISHED]

STAGE 2

Evidence Assimilation

This is where your story enters the system. We ingest unstructured data formats including:

  • Executed Contracts & Agreements
  • Digital Correspondence (Emails, WhatsApp)
  • Financial Logs & Invoices

The AI parses this data to build a chronological "Timeline of Fact".

> UPLOAD: Contract_Signed.pdf

> UPLOAD: Email_Chain_June.txt

> PARSING: "Payment made on 12/04..."

[STATUS: TIMELINE GENERATED]

STAGE 3

The Variance Audit

The core of our technology. The system overlays the Evidence (Stage 2) on top of the Legislation (Stage 1).

It looks for "Variance." Whenever an action in your timeline conflicts with a statutory requirement, it is flagged as a Confirmed Breach. This removes emotion from the equationâ€”we simply identify the gap between what was required and what occurred.

> COMPARING: Event #4 vs Statute Sec.9

> REQUIREMENT: "Must refund within 14 days"

> REALITY: "Refund issued after 32 days"

[RESULT: STATUTORY BREACH CONFIRMED]

Why This Methodology Wins

Removal of Ambiguity

By relying on hard data points (Dates, Amounts, Clauses), we strip away the "he said, she said" arguments that often stall legal proceedings.

Speed of Discovery

A human lawyer might take weeks to cross-reference 5 years of bank statements against a contract. Our engine processes this variance in minutes.
