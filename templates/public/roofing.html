The Gold Standard

Natural Slate Roofing

There is no substitute for the longevity and character of natural slate. We specialize in heritage roofing, utilizing premium Welsh Penrhyn and high-grade Spanish slates.

Our lead-work specialists ensure that every flashing, valley, and chimney apron is installed to Lead Sheet Association (LSA) standards, guaranteeing a roof that lasts over 100 years.

Residential Systems

Clay & Concrete Tiling

For modern homes and new developments, we offer precision tiling services. We work with Marley, Redland, and Sandtoft systems to provide a durable, cost-effective finish.

We install full dry-fix systems (dry ridge and verge) as standard, eliminating the need for mortar maintenance and protecting your roof against high winds.

Commercial & Industrial

Flat Roofing Solutions

We move from residential to large-scale commercial protection. Our team is certified in advanced flat roofing systems designed for warehouses, schools, and offices.

  • GRP Fibreglass: Seamless, hard-wearing protection.
  • EPDM Rubber: Flexible, single-ply sheets for large spans.
  • Torch-on Felt: Traditional multi-layer bituminous systems.
